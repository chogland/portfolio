Résumé

At the Office

I’m an ambitious Front-End Developer/UX Engineer located in St. Louis, Missouri. My focus is driven by my passion for the arts, entertainment, and the world wide web. I’ve been making websites since my early days of high school in 2001 when frames, tables, and animated GIFs were commonly used. The media has expanded a lot since I began and I do my best to continue to grow with it. I’ve been providing services since 2007 and work mostly with designers, agencies, and other freelancers. I’ve been specializing in front-end development, WordPress, and responsive design for several years now and enjoy a good challenge. JavaScript and CSS3 animations are a current favorite of mine. Code organization and semantics are extremely important to me and I usually end up spending extra time just to rewrite a module to be a little bit clearer or easier to find. I am highly efficient working from my home office and quite prefer it.
