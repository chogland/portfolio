New Thin and Light Notebooks for Spring

Remember when the only thin and light notebooks available for customers were high-priced, premium models?

Times have changed!

The 8th gen Intel® Core™ processor-powered thin and lights rolling out beginning spring 2018 (exact timing will vary by region) are powerful, versatile, and designed for every type of customer, from entry level to mainstream to premium!

What are thin and light notebooks?

Thin and light notebooks are 20 mm thick or less, with some nearly half that!

The latest thin and light designs make these notebooks an ideal choice for on-the-go customers who want convenient, easily portable devices that also deliver powerful performance and long battery life. 1

More options for more customers

For the first time, many new thin and lights will be available at entry-level and mainstream price points.

Intel has been working with manufacturers to develop notebooks that weigh less while delivering powerful performance at an affordable price point. You can see the results in the latest entry-level and mainstream thin and lights that feature SSD storage, no optical drive, aluminum and hybrid chassis, and other innovations.

Performance-class processors

The new thin and lights will be powered by 8th gen Intel® Core™ processor U SKUs, which deliver the ultra-fast, easy, and enjoyable performance customers are looking for. Plus, 8th gen Intel® Core™ processors provide immersive entertainment experiences, from 4K to gaming to virtual reality! 2
